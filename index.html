Screening Support Prototype
About This Tool

This prototype combines stool-based biomarker strip signals with clinical risk factors to provide a screening support probability. It is designed for research and development purposes only.

How It Works

1. Complete your clinical profile
2. Record the 8 strip marker values from the assay
3. View the combined risk assessment

Privacy

We value your privacy. This app runs entirely on your device — your data is never stored on our servers and will never be used for studies without your explicit consent.

Medical Disclaimer: This tool is for screening support only and does not diagnose colorectal cancer. Users with concerning results or symptoms should seek professional medical evaluation.
